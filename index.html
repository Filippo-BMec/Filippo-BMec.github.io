About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Development Economics and Local Systems (DELoS) at the University of Florence under the supervision of Marco Bellandi. I am interested in the causes and consequences of industrial agglomeration. In my dissertation, I focus on the Italian dynamics of manufacturing growth led by external economies. I am also interested in how macroeconomic trends affect the geography of production.

I was the visiting research assistant of prof. Giulio Buciuni at Trinity College Dublin in 2024. I had a research fellowship at the University of Florence from 2020 to 2021 on cultural economics and artificial intelligence.
