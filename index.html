About

I am a PhD student in Development Economics and Local Systems (DELoS). I am interested in the causes and consequences of the agglomeration of industrial activities. I use recent advances in empirical industrial organization to analyze local growth.

I was a visiting research fellow at Trinity College Dublin in 2024. I had a research fellowship at the University of Florence from 2020 to 2021 on cultural economics and artificial intelligence.
